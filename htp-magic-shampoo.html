FAQ5
Does sulfate-free clean as well?
Sulfate-free uses milder surfactants. Effective for most — oily hair may still need sulfates.
Is hair loss normal when switching?
Temporary shedding can happen. Scalp adjusts in 2–4 weeks.
Should I avoid parabens & silicones?
Both safe at regulated levels. Silicones protect; just deep-cleanse occasionally.
How often should I wash?
Oily: daily. Dry/curly: 2–3× per week. Listen to your hair.
Best for colored or damaged hair?
Sulfate-free, pH-balanced with conditioning agents. Locks pigment, reduces fade.
